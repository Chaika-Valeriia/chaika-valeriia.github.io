Luna.mind

Psychotherapy clinic that, in addition to helping existing patients, works to develop the craft of psychological care through innovative technology

Pain points

The clinic management is not up-to-date and not digitalized. No one is aware of the strengths of this clinic. No online treatment option.

Process

Research, competitor analysis, interviews with stakeholders and users, user flow, wireframes, prototypes, testing, design system, adaptive layout design, development support

Result

4 roles for convenient and modern management of clinic resources and individual and group treatment of patients. Created a design system for all teams to work flexibly, implemented accessibility rules in the product, prototype, adaptive versions.

The therapist role has access to all records. Including individual and group

Records allow you to keep a full cycle of treatment, save all the data and work for 2 types of therapists:

  • who need to use the trajectory of treatment
  • who only need patient personal information

Keep data for each individual session

Chat or video with client

Use libraries of pre-made questionnaires

Control your schedule

Create templates for documents and reuse them
